The Department Newsletter was founded by Dr. Ken Marsi in 1976 to keep past and present students and friends up to date on the most recent of changes within the department. Ken served as editor through 2001, followed by Dr. Nail Senozan from 1998 - 2004, Dr. Doug McAbee from 2005-2008 and Dr. Jeff Cohlberg from 2009 to the present.

News items, feature articles, photos, and comments are all eagerly invited, as is information on Department alumni.



Click Here to submit information directly to the Department or communicate directly with faculty members via the Faculty Directory. When sending an alumnus update, please be sure to indicate your CSULB degree, major, and year of graduation.
